About me

A bit about who I am

I got into digital marketing because I liked seeing measurable outcomes. Campaigns that either work or don't — there's no ambiguity. Over time I've gotten good at figuring out why they work, tweaking them, and keeping costs in check while hitting business goals.

What I do

I plan and run paid campaigns on Meta Ads and Google Ads. That includes everything from audience research and budget allocation to A/B testing creatives and reading performance data to make better decisions.

Where I'm headed

I'm currently building my knowledge in programmatic advertising — learning how DSPs, RTB, and platforms like DV360 and The Trade Desk work. My goal is to move into a Programmatic Media Buyer role over time.

How I work

I keep things data-driven. I use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, and GTM regularly. I work closely with creative and landing page teams to make sure every part of a campaign is lined up for better conversions.

What I care about

Clear results, honest reporting, and campaigns that improve over time. I believe good marketing comes from understanding the audience and testing consistently — not just launching and hoping for the best.
